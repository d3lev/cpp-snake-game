# SIMPLE SNAKE (C++) by d3lev

## Project Overview:

A simple snake game based off of a video tutorial and a raylib template.
Video: 

## Features/Gameplay:

Extremely barebones and simple. has a score counter on the bottom right of the border.
Features a game over screen when you die.

## How to play:

1. Launch "main.exe"
2. Enjoy!

## Known bugs:

1. instant death when pressing A, S and D simultaneously, even in the beginning where it should be impossible to die to your own tail.

## Future Improvements:
